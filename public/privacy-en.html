🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

Last Updated: January 13, 2026
Version: 1.0

1. Introduction

Welcome to TuyJo's Privacy Policy. TuyJo is a private messaging application designed specifically for couples, featuring advanced end-to-end encryption (RSA-2048 + AES-256).

Your privacy is fundamental to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have over your personal data.

🔐 Core Principle

Zero-Knowledge Architecture: Thanks to end-to-end encryption, your messages, photos, and documents are readable ONLY by you and your partner. Not even we, the developers, can access your content.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Authentication Data

  • Firebase User ID: A unique identifier automatically generated by Firebase Authentication
  • Email (optional): If you choose to associate an email with your account
  • RSA Public Key: Your public encryption key (not secret), stored to allow your partner to encrypt messages for you

2.2 Pairing Data

  • Partner ID: Your partner's Firebase identifier linked to your account
  • Pairing Status: Information about the connection status between devices

2.3 Messages and Content (Encrypted)

  • Encrypted Messages: Message text encrypted with AES-256
  • Encrypted Attachments: End-to-end encrypted photos, videos, and documents
  • TODOs and Reminders: Shared reminders and notes (encrypted)
  • Metadata: Sending timestamp, message ID, message type (text/attachment/TODO)
Important Note: All content (messages, attachments, TODOs) is end-to-end encrypted. We only store encrypted data that we cannot read without your private keys, which are stored ONLY on your device.

2.4 Technical and Usage Data

  • FCM Token: Token for Firebase Cloud Messaging push notifications
  • Error Logs: Information about app crashes and errors (anonymized)
  • Usage Statistics: Aggregated and anonymous app usage data

3. How We Use Data

3.1 Primary Purposes

  • Provide the Service: Synchronize messages between your devices and your partner's
  • Notifications: Send push notifications when you receive new messages or reminders
  • Security: Maintain the integrity and security of the pairing system
  • Improvements: Fix bugs and improve app features

3.2 What We DON'T Do with Your Data

  • ❌ We don't read your messages (impossible due to E2E encryption)
  • ❌ We don't sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ We don't use your data for advertising or profiling
  • ❌ We don't share your content with anyone (except legal cases, see sec. 5)

4. Encryption and Security

4.1 Encryption Architecture

TuyJo uses a two-tier encryption system:

  • RSA-2048: For secure exchange of public keys during pairing
  • AES-256: For encrypting messages and attachments
  • Unique Key per Message: Each message has a randomly generated unique AES key

🔑 Your Private Keys

Your RSA private keys are stored EXCLUSIVELY on your device using flutter_secure_storage, an encrypted storage system protected by the operating system (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android). Not even we can access them.

4.2 Security Measures

  • Firestore Security Rules: Strict access rules preventing unauthorized reading
  • HTTPS/TLS: All communications between app and server use secure connections
  • Cloud Storage Rules: Attachments are only accessible to authorized users (even though encrypted)
  • Secure Backup: You can safely export and import your keys

5. Data Sharing with Third Parties

5.1 Services Used

TuyJo uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Messaging
    • Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
Important: Even Firebase only stores encrypted data. Google can see metadata (timestamps, data size) but cannot read the content of your messages.

5.2 Legal Disclosure

We may disclose unencrypted data (such as user ID, metadata) if:

  • Required by legal orders (warrants, subpoenas)
  • Necessary to protect our legal rights
  • Required to prevent illegal activities or fraud

Note: We cannot disclose encrypted message content because we don't have the keys to decrypt it.

6. Data Retention

6.1 Retention Period

  • Messages: Stored indefinitely until you manually delete them
  • Attachments: Stored indefinitely until you delete them
  • Account: Data stored while account is active
  • Technical Logs: Stored for maximum 90 days

6.2 Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Delete Single Messages: Long press on a message → Delete
  • Delete Attachments: Delete messages containing attachments
  • Delete All Data: Settings → "Delete All Data" (irreversible)
  • Delete Account: Contact us for complete account deletion

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: You can correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: You can export your data (backup feature)
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data
  • Right to Complaint: You can file a complaint with the data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address indicated in section 9.

8. Children's Privacy

TuyJo is not intended for users under 16 years of age (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you are a parent and discover that your child has provided us with personal data, contact us for immediate removal.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of substantial changes through in-app notification or email. The "Last Updated" date at the top of the page indicates when the policy was last modified.

Previous Versions: You can request previous versions of this policy by contacting us.
